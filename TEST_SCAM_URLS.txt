HOW TO TEST
===========

1. Copy any of these URLs into the message area
2. Click "Scan Message" or "Check URL"
3. The detector should flag them as:
   - CRITICAL: URL shorteners, punycode
   - HIGH: Typosquatting, impersonation, encoded, @ symbol, long URLs
   - MEDIUM: Excessive subdomains, dashes, numeric, mixed case
   - LOW: Free TLDs, suspicious keywords

EXPECTED RESULTS
================

✅ All CRITICAL risk URLs should show CRITICAL risk level
✅ All HIGH risk URLs should show HIGH or CRITICAL risk level
✅ All MEDIUM risk URLs should show MEDIUM or higher
✅ Multiple indicators (dashes + typo) should compound risk

If URLs are NOT being detected, it may indicate:
- Regex pattern issues
- Risk scoring not working properly
- Detection logic needing refinement
