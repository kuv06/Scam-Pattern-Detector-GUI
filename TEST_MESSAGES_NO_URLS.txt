SECTION 1: CRITICAL RISK MESSAGES (5+ indicators)
================================================================================

TEST 1.1 - URGENT + ACCOUNT + PASSWORD + LEGAL ACTION
Message: URGENT! Your account has been suspended due to unusual activity. Confirm your password immediately or legal action will be taken. This is your final notice!
Expected Risk: CRITICAL - DEFINITE SCAM
Detected Patterns:
  - Urgency/Pressure: "URGENT", "immediately"
  - Account Security: "account has been suspended", "unusual activity"
  - Personal Info Request: "Confirm your password"
  - Threat/Fear Tactics: "legal action will be taken", "final notice"

TEST 1.2 - PRIZE SCAM + LOTTERY + VERIFICATION + TOO GOOD TO BE TRUE
Message: Congratulations! You have won a $1,000,000 lottery prize! Click to claim your reward instantly. We need to verify your identity by confirming your SSN and bank account details. 100% guaranteed, no catch!
Expected Risk: CRITICAL - DEFINITE SCAM
Detected Patterns:
  - Prize/Lottery: "You have won", "lottery prize"
  - Personal Info Request: "verify your identity", "SSN and bank account"
  - Too Good to Be True: "100% guaranteed", "no catch"
  - Phishing Link: "Click to claim"

TEST 1.3 - AUTHORITY IMPERSONATION + THREAT + URGENCY + PAYMENT DEMAND
Message: This is the IRS. Your account is under investigation for tax evasion. Wire $5,000 immediately to settle your debt or we will file criminal charges and arrest you. Act now or face consequences!

QUICK REFERENCE: RISK LEVELS & WARNING BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

CRITICAL (5+ indicators):
  → RED panel with "CRITICAL - DEFINITE SCAM"
  → WARNING dialog: "High Risk Message"
  → Recommendation: DO NOT RESPOND! Delete immediately.

HIGH (3-4 indicators):
  → ORANGE panel with "HIGH RISK - LIKELY SCAM"
  → WARNING dialog: "High Risk Message"
  → Recommendation: Very suspicious. Do not click links or provide information.

MEDIUM (2 indicators):
  → YELLOW panel with "MEDIUM RISK - POSSIBLE SCAM"
  → WARNING dialog: "Medium Risk Message"
  → Recommendation: Be very cautious. Verify sender through official channels.

LOW (1 indicator):
  → BLUE panel with "LOW RISK - SUSPICIOUS"
  → INFO dialog: "Low Risk Message"
  → Recommendation: Contains suspicious elements. Exercise caution.

SAFE (0 indicators):
  → GREEN panel with "NO SCAM DETECTED"
  → NO warning dialog
  → Recommendation: No obvious scam patterns found.

================================================================================
TESTING TIPS
================================================================================

1. Copy a test message above.
2. Paste into the GUI's input area.
3. Click "Scan Message" (NOT "Check URL" since these have no URLs).
4. Verify the colored risk panel matches expected level.
5. Check the warning dialog appears or doesn't appear as expected.
6. Review the detected patterns in the results area.
7. Click "Clear" and try the next message.
8. Test edge cases to see if they produce false positives.

Expected Behavior:
  ✓ CRITICAL/HIGH messages show red/orange panel + WARNING dialog
  ✓ MEDIUM messages show yellow panel + WARNING dialog
  ✓ LOW messages show blue panel + INFO dialog
  ✓ SAFE messages show green panel + NO dialog
  ✓ All detected patterns listed in "OTHER DETECTED INDICATORS"
  ✓ Dialogs are clear and actionable
  ✓ Recommendations match risk level
